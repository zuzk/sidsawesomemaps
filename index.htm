This is my third map, Survival with a twist!
This is a survival map that uses trading!


In this map you mine an infinitely regenerating stone that you trade with Villagers for gold and other supplies to survive!


In this map you can do get and do most
of the things you would normally do! Like...
going to the Nether,
building cool stuff, 
fighting off mobs,
 mining (of course),
 playing with friends (if you have any),
 and just plain having fun!
