This is my second map, Trapped Survival

In this map you  are spawned
in a little bedrock area with no
escape! You are given the
choice to pick one on many 
classes! Each one has its own 
special ability's and Items!

Choose carefully! :)

Once you choose you get teleported  
to a Huge bedrock area!
Also with no escape!
Now you have to survive 
using your supplies and the
materials around you!

Good Luck! ;)
