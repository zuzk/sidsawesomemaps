Install Instructions For Optifine

#1 Make sure you have Java installed.

#2 Make sure you have run Minecraft version 1.8.4 at least once

(If you haven't then click here for instructions on how to do that)

#3 Double click the file

#4 Click the "Install" button

#5 Double click on your Minecraft icon

#6 Go to the launcher and click on the profile you want to make Optifine 1.8.4

#7 Click the "Edit Profile" icon

#8 Under the "Use Version" tab click on the down arrow and select "release 1.8.4-Optifine_HD_U_D4"

#9 On the bottom of the window click "Save Profile"

#10 Make sure you have that profile selected then press "Play"

#11 Wait for it to load, then once Minecraft is fully launched click "Quit Game"

#12 Now whenever you want to play Optifine 1.8.4 all you need to do is use that profile!

#13 Your done!
